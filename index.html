Type or speak -- Sam understands both

"Who owes me money?" → Sam shows your three overdue invoices totaling $4,200 and offers to send reminders right now.

"Schedule a drain cleaning for Sarah Kim tomorrow at 2pm" → Sam finds the customer, creates the job, and shows you a confirmation card. One tap to confirm.

"How did we do this week?" → Sam gives you a 30-second rundown of revenue, jobs, reviews, and anything that needs your attention.
